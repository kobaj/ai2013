Q. Do we both have to submit or just one person on the team?
A. You only need to submit one project per group.  So, your group should pick someone to be responsible for the submissions.

Q. Can we switch which ladder we compete in for a project?
A. You should pick a single ladder for a given project, but you can switch from project to project.

Q. Whats the resubmit process for changed or updated code?
A. Yes you do the same process to resubmit a project as you did for the initial submission.
